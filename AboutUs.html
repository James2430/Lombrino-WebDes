The HOODHAVEN's History

HoodHaven was founded in 2024 by James Cunanan Lombrino. The company started as a passion project to provide stylish and comfortable hoodies to the local community. Over the years, HoodHaven has grown to become a leading provider of high-quality apparel, delivering exceptional customer service and a wide range of designs to customers across the region.

The Origins of HoodHaven: A Founder's Lifelong Obsession with Hoodies The story of HoodHaven begins with its founder, James Cunanan Lombrino, and their lifelong love affair with hoodies. From a young age, James was utterly captivated by the simple yet versatile garment, amassing an ever-growing collection of hoodies in every color, style, and material imaginable. "I just couldn't get enough of them," James reminisces. "There was something about the comfort, the coziness, the way a hoodie could instantly make you feel relaxed and at ease. It was my go-to piece for everything, from lounging at home to going out with friends." As James grew older, their passion for hoodies only intensified. They scoured stores and online retailers, searching endlessly for the perfect hoodie that checked all the boxes - superior quality, flattering fit, and that unbeatable sense of warmth and security. But more often than not, James would come up empty-handed, disappointed by the lack of truly exceptional hoodies on the market. "I'd find hoodies that looked great, but they'd fall apart after a few wears. Or they'd be well-made, but the fit just wouldn't work for me," James explains. "I knew there had to be a way to create a hoodie that combined top-notch construction with a style and comfort level that would make it an everyday staple." And so, in the early of this year, James decided to take matters into their own hands. Driven by their lifelong passion and an unwavering determination to fill the void in the market, they set out to launch their own apparel brand - one that would redefine the humble hoodie and elevate it to new heights of quality and desirability. Thus, HoodHaven was born, a testament to the transformative power of a single, deceptively simple garment. From those early days of relentless prototyping and fabric experimentation, the brand has grown into a globally recognized lifestyle empire, all while staying true to its founder's original vision of creating the perfect hoodie. "It's been an incredible journey," James reflects. "I never could have imagined that my love for hoodies would one day inspire the creation of a whole company. But I'm so grateful that I followed my passion and took a chance. HoodHaven is my life's work, and I'm honored to share it with the world."
